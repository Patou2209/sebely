Electrical Works, Renewable Energy, and Energy Efficiency Solutions

Sebely carries out low- and medium-voltage electrical works, integrating requirements for performance, safety, and sustainability. Our expertise covers both conventional infrastructure and innovative solutions in renewable energy and energy efficiency. In addition to general contracting services, Sebely also offers EPC (Engineering, Procurement & Construction) projects, ensuring full project management from design to commissioning. Through an integrated approach, we support our clients in delivering reliable projects tailored to local contexts and compliant with current standards.

Our services include:

Photovoltaic Power Plants: Supply, installation, and commissioning of solar systems for decentralized electricity generation.

Construction of MV and LV Lines: Development of medium- and low-voltage distribution networks in urban and rural areas.

Construction of MV Substations and LV Cabins: Installation of transformation and distribution facilities for secure network supply.

Industrial and Building Installations: Design and execution of electrical systems for industrial, commercial, and institutional sites.

Deployment of Energy-Saving Solutions: Implementation of energy-efficient technologies (high-performance lighting, optimized HVAC systems, variable speed drives, etc.).
