Datacenter & Telecommunication

our tailored solutions to meet your unique needs

Leveraging our extensive knowledge and experience in ICT infrastructure, we propose tailored innovative solutions that meet the unique needs of each project. We offer a wide range of services from site due diligence to operation and maintenance (O&M).

Site Due Diligence:

Our site due diligence service for data center projects is designed to ensure that your investment is strategically sound and future-ready. We conduct comprehensive assessments that include site analysis, risk assessment, regulatory compliance, and infrastructure assessment providing you with critical insights to make informed decisions. Our expert team collaborates closely with clients to identify potential risks and opportunities, ensuring that every site is optimized for performance, scalability, and sustainability. Trust us to lay the groundwork for your data center’s success.

Data Center Assessment and Audit:

Facility Audit:

This service aims to detect deficiencies and problems in any critical facility and to recommend proactive measures and facility upgrading in response to, or even before, major disruptions. Our detailed health check to the electrical and cooling infrastructures enables us to determine system availability and reliability. We also provide a detailed report with recommendations for improvement and a roadmap for implementation.

Audit for Existing MSC, BSC and Datacenters:

  • Site inspection and survey
  • Technical review of the existing electrical and cooling distribution systems
  • Reliability and availability analysis
  • Single point of failure analysis

Facilities management:

Sebely provides a reliable, integrated management system that helps ensure safety, high availability and maximum energy efficiency of equipment in mission critical facilities. Our services include maintenance and monitoring of electrical, cooling, and fire systems, document management and achiving (diagrams, equipment labels, etc.), technical advisory, and so on.
