About us




Established in 2020, Sebely is a multi-disciplinary consulting engineering company that provides advisory, design, delivery, and asset management services in Sub-Saharan Africa.

Our core services include:

  • Control and Instrumentation Engineering
  • Energy, Water, and Carbon Management
  • Capacity Development
  • Asset Management

Fostered by people of excellence, with a strong focus on delivering best-in-class services, our solutions are tailored to meet the needs of our clients.

Our Vision

At Sebely, we strive to provide our customers with the highest level of satisfaction, giving them access to state-of-the-art, cost-effective, highly efficient and sustainable services and products. We aim to be the preferred partner in the provision of engineering services in Sub-Saharan Africa.
