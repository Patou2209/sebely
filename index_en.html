Welcome to Sebely

Your Partner in Excellence

Contact Us Discover Our Services

Founded in 2020, Sebely is both a design office and an engineering company specializing in electrical and energy engineering. We combine technical analysis rigor with on-the-ground operational capacity to deliver comprehensive, tailor-made solutions for electrification projects in sub-Saharan Africa.

Our strength lies in our dual expertise. Upstream, our design office conducts in-depth analyses, feasibility studies, technical designs, and development plans tailored to local contexts. Downstream, we handle project implementation, including construction, commissioning, installation certification, infrastructure management, and maintenance. With a highly skilled team and deep understanding of African realities, we support our clients at every stage of their project lifecycle.

Our approach is built on three core pillars:

  • Integrated Technical Expertise: a multidisciplinary team capable of seamlessly transitioning from study to execution with consistency and efficiency.
  • Consultative Vision: active listening to client needs to propose relevant, scalable, and sustainable solutions.
  • Responsible Commitment: projects designed to optimize resources, control costs, and ensure long-term performance.

    • At Sebely, we don’t just design or build — we conceive, deliver, and sustain tomorrow’s energy infrastructure, forging partnerships based on trust, excellence, and lasting impact.

    Consulting

    Sebely offers a full range of technical consulting services, designed to support project owners, donors, and public or private operators in structuring and securing their energy projects. Our design office conducts feasibility studies, preliminary (APS) and detailed (APD) design phases, integrating technical, economic, environmental, and regulatory constraints specific to each context. These studies define viable, optimized solutions tailored to local realities.

    We also handle the preparation of tender documents (DAO), technical assistance in procurement processes, supervision and control of works, and certification of electrical installations in compliance with current standards. Sebely also supports energy sector stakeholders in developing strategic tariff studies, essential for the economic viability of electricity distribution and commercialization projects.

    Construction

    Sebely delivers complete energy infrastructure projects, leveraging its technical expertise and on-site operational capacity. We build photovoltaic solar power plants as well as facilities based on other renewable energy technologies, tailored to local needs and energy transition goals. Our teams also handle the construction of HV, MV, and LV lines and substations, ensuring compliance with standards, installation safety, and network reliability.

    Beyond energy infrastructure, Sebely constructs industrial facilities and technical buildings, integrating electrical systems, automation, and project-specific equipment. Our integrated approach enables us to deliver turnkey projects optimized for performance, durability, and ease of maintenance. Every site is managed with rigor, adhering to deadlines, budgets, and the highest quality standards.

    Infrastructure Management

    Through its Sebely Facility Management division, Sebely provides technical and operational management of energy infrastructure, with a focus on electrical systems in commercial and industrial environments. Our services include preventive and corrective maintenance, as well as daily equipment monitoring, ensuring reliability and performance.

    In addition, we manage other critical systems related to safety, comfort, and protection of buildings and industrial facilities. Sebely Facility Management also supplies and installs new equipment to meet expansion, modernization, or replacement needs. This comprehensive approach ensures operational continuity, long-term performance, and adaptation to technological advancements.
