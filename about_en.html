About Us

Your Trusted Partner in Engineering

Who We Are

Founded in 2020 and based in the Democratic Republic of Congo, Sebely is a multidisciplinary engineering company driven by a team of passionate and committed experts focused on excellence. Specializing in energy, telecommunications, and resource efficiency, Sebely delivers tailor-made technical solutions that meet the specific needs of its clients. With deep local knowledge and a rigorous approach, the company has established itself as a key player in the design, implementation, and management of complex infrastructure.

Sebely’s Vision

Our vision is to become the leading engineering partner in the sub-region, delivering innovative, sustainable, and high-value solutions. We aim to transform technical challenges into opportunities for development, actively contributing to infrastructure modernization and improving quality of life. At Sebely, we believe in responsible engineering that serves progress and creates positive impact.

Sebely’s Mission

Sebely’s mission is to design, build, and maintain reliable, cost-effective, and sustainable technical solutions, with a focus on client satisfaction and operational excellence. We are committed to supporting our partners at every stage of their projects, combining local expertise with international standards. Through our divisions, including Sebely Facility Management, we work to create high-performing, resilient infrastructure aligned with current and future challenges.
