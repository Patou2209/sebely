Consulting

Technical expertise, strategy, and innovation at the service of your engineering and infrastructure projects.

Our Expertise at Your Disposal

Accredited for intellectual services in the electricity sector, Sebely is recognized for its technical expertise and methodological rigor. This official recognition allows us to operate in full compliance on strategic missions, from preliminary analysis to specialized technical assistance, within the framework of complex and structuring energy projects.

Our design office offers a full range of consulting services, designed to support project owners, donors, and public or private operators in structuring, planning, and securing their projects. Thanks to our in-depth knowledge of the regional environment, we integrate the technical, economic, regulatory, and social realities specific to each context to propose viable and tailored solutions.

Electricity Generation, Transmission, and Distribution

Generation: Sebely provides specialized consulting services, with a strong focus on renewable energy, particularly solar photovoltaic and hydropower. Our design office supports project developers in analyzing energy potential, selecting appropriate technologies, technical design, and evaluating the economic and environmental aspects of proposed solutions. With our local expertise and mastery of energy transition challenges, we contribute to implementing sustainable, high-performance projects adapted to on-the-ground realities.

Transmission: Sebely assists clients in designing and optimizing energy transit infrastructure, including high- and medium-voltage lines and transformer substations. Our consulting services cover technical studies, route analysis, impact assessments, as well as support in procurement and construction supervision. With our command of standards and local requirements, we help establish reliable, secure networks tailored to growing energy demands.

Distribution: We work on low- and medium-voltage networks in both urban and rural areas. Sebely conducts sizing, rehabilitation, and expansion studies, integrating service quality, loss reduction, and equitable access to electricity. Our services also include developing master plans, investment planning, and technical support to operators to improve network performance and resilience.

Electricity Commercialization

Sebely supports energy sector stakeholders in structuring electricity commercialization models, offering strategic and technical consulting services.

Tariff Studies: Development of balanced tariff structures compliant with regulatory frameworks, incorporating operating costs, consumption profiles, and accessibility goals.

Demand Studies: Analysis of current and future energy needs, user segmentation, consumption profile modeling, and demand growth forecasting.

Financial Modeling: Simulation of economic flows related to electricity distribution, profitability and risk assessment, and support in structuring economic models.

Commercial Management Support: Advisory on implementing or optimizing billing, collection, and subscriber management systems.

Regulatory and Strategic Analysis: Guidance on understanding and integrating legislative frameworks, tariff policies, and subsidy mechanisms.

Energy and Environmental Resource Management

Sebely offers integrated consulting services for the optimized management of energy and environmental resources, with a focus on sustainable performance. We assist our clients in controlling electricity consumption, rational water management, and carbon emission reduction, while considering technical, economic, and regulatory constraints.

Energy Audits: Detailed assessment of facilities to identify sources of waste and propose energy efficiency measures.

Electricity Consumption Optimization: Analysis of consumption profiles and technical recommendations to reduce costs and improve performance.

Rational Water Management: Studies and advice for efficient use of water resources in industrial and commercial environments.

Carbon Emission Reduction: Development of decarbonization strategies, monitoring of emission indicators, and support for regulatory compliance.

Environmental Performance Monitoring: Implementation of tracking and reporting systems to manage sustainability goals.

Measurement & Verification: Application of recognized protocols to quantify energy savings achieved through efficiency actions, in accordance with international standards.

Data Centers

We support data center project developers during critical planning and technical security phases. Leveraging our expertise in electrical infrastructure and deep knowledge of the local environment, we provide strategic support to ensure reliability, compliance, and performance from the design stage. Our services include:

Site Selection Due Diligence: Technical and environmental evaluation of potential locations, considering energy, logistical, and regulatory constraints.

Electrification Studies: Analysis of power supply needs, network availability, infrastructure sizing, and definition of suitable supply solutions.

Technical Audits of Facilities: Verification of compliance, safety, and performance of existing or in-development electrical systems.
